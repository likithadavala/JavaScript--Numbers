Welcome to Nykaa

Nykaa is one of India's leading beauty, skincare, haircare, wellness, and fashion e-commerce companies. Founded in 2012 by Falguni Nayar, the company offers a wide range of products from both international and Indian brands through its website, mobile app,and physical retail stores. Nykaa aims to provide customers with authentic,high-quality products and expert beauty advice. Over the years,it has become a trusted destination for beauty and fashion shopping in India, known for its extensive product selection,attractive offers, and convenient shopping experience.
